Green Shield Harvest

This project harnesses the power of IoT and Deep Learning to revolutionize hydroponic agriculture. By automating all tasks typically done by a farmer, this system ensures an efficient and sustainable process, optimizing plant growth and maximizing output. The system also generates a dataset of images to analyze plant health over time, contributing to the research on smart farming.
